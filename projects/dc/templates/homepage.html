Spacebook is a social networking web 2.0 project created to support the NASA Science Mission Directorate and the rest of NASA. It is built using an aggregation of open source tools including Python, Django, Pinax, and a lot of other projects. By leveraging these tools and the support of their communities, we have had the fortune of being able to steer the direction of these tools in direction that more suits the needs of NASA and the Federal Government at large.
